# Cylindr

Cylindr is a zero-cost, community-driven Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to track LPG gas cylinder availability across local neighborhoods in real-time. It connects consumers actively searching for gas with distributors that have verified inventory, solving the chaotic "call every agency" problem during peak shortages.

### 🏠 For Consumers
* **Hyper-Local Tracking:** See real-time LPG availability (In Stock, Low, Out) mapped exactly to your GPS location.
* **Smart Recommendations:** Don't guess where to go. The app actively calculates the "Best Option Right Now" based on distance, update freshness, and crowd-verified data.
* **Instant Alerts:** Get an automatic push notification the absolute second an empty agency restocks.
* **Frictionless Checking:** One-tap to filter for your specific cylinder size (14.2kg, 19kg, or 5kg) and see accepted payments (UPI or Cash).

### 🚚 For Distributors & Agency Owners
* **Deflect Unnecessary Calls:** Update your stock status to the live map in just one tap, significantly reducing the hundreds of phone calls asking "Is gas available?".
* **Drive Targeted Foot Traffic:** Checkboxes for "Walk-ins Accepted" or "UPI Accepted" ensure the customers who arrive are ready to transact under your rules. 
* **Self-Onboarding:** Not on the map? Register your exact GPS coordinates and agency details directly from your phone in under 60 seconds.

## Technology Stack
* Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Leaflet.js
* Backend & Serverless: Firebase Firestore, Firebase Functions, Firebase Auth
